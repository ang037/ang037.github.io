Anshu Gupta

PhD Student
University of California San Diego
I am a PhD student at University of California, San Diego majoring in Computer Science and Engineering.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Architecture and Computational Genomics. I am a part of Turakhia Lab under the supervision of Professor Yatish Turakhia where I work on solving computational challenges and develop efficient hardware for genomic data analysis.

I completed my undergraduate in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from IIEST Shibpur, West Bengal, India. After completing my undergraduate, I worked as a Digital Design Engineer at Analog Devices, where I was involved in Digital Design and Implementation of industry-standard SoCs with a successful tape-out experience. I also got awarded with DAAD WISE Scholarship and was a summer research intern at the University of Bremen, Germany.
