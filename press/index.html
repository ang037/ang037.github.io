Media coverage of my research, primarily around the ROADIES paper published in PNAS.

About the Coverage
The media coverage below was triggered by the publication of "Accurate, scalable, and fully automated inference of species trees from raw genome assemblies using ROADIES" in Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS) — selected as the journal cover article of Vol. 122, No. 19, May 13, 2025. PNAS has an acceptance rate of ~15% and is the second most-cited scientific journal in the world (Impact Factor: 9.1).
